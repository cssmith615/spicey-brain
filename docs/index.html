🧠 Spicey Brain

Privacy Policy

Short version: Spicey Brain stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Your child's data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

1. Who We Are

Spicey Brain is a routine management and ADHD support app for families. It is designed to help parents manage daily routines, track mood and behavior, coordinate with professionals, and support children with ADHD and similar conditions.

2. Data We Collect

Spicey Brain does not collect any data from you. All information you enter into the app — including child profiles, routines, mood entries, medication logs, and professional notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

We do not have servers that receive your data. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We are aware that this app involves data about children. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • All child data entered into the app is stored locally on the parent's device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Parents are in full control of all data at all times.

4. AI Insights Feature

The AI Insights feature uses the Anthropic Claude API. If you choose to use this feature, you must provide your own Anthropic API key. When you generate insights:

  • A summary of anonymised behavioral and routine data is sent to Anthropic's API.
  • No names, exact ages, or identifying information are included in the prompt.
  • Your API key is stored securely on your device using the operating system's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and is never transmitted to us.
  • Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API requests. You can review it at anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Professional Portal

The Professional Portal allows you to share a secure access code with teachers, therapists, or caregivers. Portal access is controlled entirely by you:

  • You create and revoke access codes at any time.
  • Notes submitted by professionals are stored locally on your device.
  • Portal codes do not communicate with external servers — access is validated locally.

6. Report Export

The Export Report feature generates a PDF on your device. You can choose to share it via any app on your phone (email, messaging, etc.). We do not receive or store any exported reports.

7. Notifications

Spicey Brain may request permission to send local push notifications for routine reminders and medication alerts. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

8. Data Deletion

All app data can be deleted at any time by going to Settings → Reset App Data within the app, or by uninstalling the app from your device. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date below and notify users through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at: privacy@spiceybrain.app
