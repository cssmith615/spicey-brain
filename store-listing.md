## Full Description (Both stores — max 4000 chars)

Spicey Brain is the all-in-one family support app built for parents raising children with ADHD. Manage daily routines, track mood and behaviour, coordinate with teachers and therapists, and celebrate every win — all in one place.

**FOR PARENTS**
• Build morning, afternoon, and evening routines with step-by-step missions
• Track mood, focus, and behaviour patterns over time
• Log medications and set dose reminders
• Communicate directly with teachers via the built-in Teacher Log
• Review AI-powered insights that identify patterns in your child's data
• Export professional PDF reports to share with your child's care team
• Invite therapists and teachers to a secure Professional Portal

**FOR KIDS**
• A fun, dedicated child view with their own avatar
• Complete missions one at a time with timers and encouragement
• Earn stars for every completed routine
• Redeem stars for rewards set by parents

**FOR PROFESSIONALS**
• Secure portal access via a shareable code
• Add session notes, observations, and recommendations
• View the child's routine schedule and progress
• Send intake questionnaires for parents to complete

**PRIVACY FIRST**
All data is stored locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or sell your family's information. No accounts required.
